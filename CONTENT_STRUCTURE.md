#### How It Works Section
1. Your college gets you in
2. Build your portfolio
3. Get discovered on merit

#### Why BloomingPros.ai is Different
- **Skill-first, not resume-first:** Your portfolio shows companies what you can do — not just your CGPA or college name.
- **Start in earlier Year, not just final year:** The earlier you build, the stronger your portfolio by placement season.
- **Not just Tier-1 colleges:** Talent is everywhere. Our platform gives every student the same visibility tools.
- **Verified by your institution:** Your profile carries institutional credibility, which companies trust.

#### Who is this for?
Currently, BloomingPros.ai focuses on engineering and technology students for the PoC. If your college is a partner, you're eligible. Support for other streams — business, design, commerce — is coming after PoC.
